ABOUT GRADIENT

At Gradient we develop advanced mobile photo editing technologies. Our goal is to make photo editing easy and accessible
for everyone whether they are professionals finishing their new portfolio album or beginners whose photo creating journey has just begun.
The company was established by Vladyslav Urazov and Bogdan Matveev, artificial intelligence and machine learning enthusiasts.
